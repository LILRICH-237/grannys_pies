Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

Ingredients

    1 cup white sugar
    2 tbsp flour
    3 tbsp cornstarch
    1/4 tsp salt

Directions

Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

Make Filling: Combine sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt.

Prepare Crust: Bake crust until golden brown.

Finish: Pour filling into crust and bake.

Enjoy your delicious cake pie!
